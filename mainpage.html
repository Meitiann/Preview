    Our Services

    Alvin Heng Engineering and building Services is a one-stop electrical contracting firm that provides a complete range of services for electrical and security systems.
    We are BCA registrated and our core expertise is in Electrical Installation for design-and-build condominium housing development projects.

    Background of the company

    Alvin Heng Engineering & Building Company is a business company that provides one-step solution for Facilities Services,Addition and Alteration Work and Reinstatement Work for its clients.
    Alvin Heng Engineering & Building was initially established as "Progressive Design and Contracts" in 2013 with its core focus of providing handyman services for residential and commercial market.The company was then renamed as "Alvin Heng Engineering & Building Services with it revamp focus of providing mantainence services.In 2016, Alvin Heng Engineering Building started to expand to a complete set of facilities services which provides mechanical and electrical maintenance services and handyman services. In 2017, the services further expand into providing churn project work as its addition; value-add services to its clients on top of facilities maintenance and personal information of the founder.
